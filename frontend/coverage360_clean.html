Which plans cover Rituximab for RA?
UnitedHealth and Priority Health cover Rituximab for rheumatoid arthritis with prior authorization. Cigna requires a biosimilar step-therapy trial first, effective Feb 2026.
Sources: UHC MP-02.093 · Cigna CPB 0656 · PH Drug List 2026
What changed at UHC this quarter?
UHC reduced their prior chemo requirement from 2 regimens to 1, added biosimilar step therapy, and restricted site of care to ambulatory infusion centers. Effective Jan 1, 2026.
Source: UHC Policy MP-02.093 · Q4 2025 update bulletin
